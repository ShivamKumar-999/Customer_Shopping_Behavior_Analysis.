📌 Project Title

Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard using Power BI

📊 Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to understand customer purchasing patterns, subscription behavior, and revenue distribution across different segments. This lack of insight can lead to ineffective marketing strategies and missed revenue opportunities.

🎯 Objective

To analyze customer data and build an interactive dashboard that provides insights into purchasing behavior, revenue trends, and customer segmentation to support data-driven decision-making.

🗂️ Dataset
Source: Simulated / Kaggle dataset
Size: ~3,900 customers, multiple features
Key Columns:
Customer ID
Purchase Amount
Product Category (Clothing, Accessories, Footwear, Outerwear)
Age Group
Subscription Status
Review Rating
Gender
Shipping Type



🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI (Dashboard & Data Visualization)
Python (Pandas, NumPy – for preprocessing, if used)
Excel / CSV (Data source)
DAX (Calculated measures & KPIs)



🔍 Key Steps / Methodology
Cleaned and structured raw customer data
Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Created calculated metrics:
Total Customers
Average Purchase Amount
Average Review Rating
Built interactive filters for:
Subscription Status
Gender
Category
Shipping Type
Designed multiple visualizations:
Sales by Category
Revenue by Category
Subscription Distribution
Revenue by Age Group
Sales by Age Group
Developed a user-friendly, interactive dashboard layout



📈 Key Insights / Results
Clothing category generates the highest sales and revenue
Majority of customers (~73%) are non-subscribers, indicating growth potential
Young Adults contribute the highest revenue and sales volume
Average purchase amount is $59.76, indicating mid-range spending behavior
Customer satisfaction is moderate with an average rating of 3.75



📊 Visualizations / Dashboard
KPI cards (Total Customers, Avg Purchase, Avg Rating)
Bar charts (Sales & Revenue by Category)
Donut chart (Subscription Status)
Horizontal bar charts (Age Group Analysis)
Interactive filters (Gender, Category, Shipping Type)



🚀 Business Impact
Helps identify high-performing product categories
Reveals untapped subscription opportunities
Supports targeted marketing strategies for key age groups
Enables better inventory and sales planning
Improves customer segmentation and personalization
